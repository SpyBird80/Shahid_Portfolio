Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 1-3 months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to ensure your website continues to perform optimally. This includes updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. We can discuss the level of support that best fits your needs.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need to understand your goals, target audience, and project requirements. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, review any existing materials, and determine the scope of work. I'll also need access to any content, images, or branding materials you have.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Absolutely! I work with clients from all over the world. Thanks to modern communication tools and project management platforms, I can effectively collaborate with international clients. All projects are managed through secure online platforms to ensure smooth communication and file sharing.

What's your payment process?

For new projects, I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon completion. For ongoing services, I offer flexible billing options including monthly retainers. Payments can be made via bank transfer or credit card through secure payment processors.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

Yes, I can help with on-page SEO optimization as part of website development. For comprehensive digital marketing strategies, I work with trusted partners who specialize in SEO, content marketing, and paid advertising. I can coordinate with these specialists to ensure your project aligns with your marketing goals.
